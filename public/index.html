About Carvèl

Designed for families
who cannot always be there

Carvèl addresses a growing real-world challenge — working children who cannot be physically present to monitor the health of their aging parents. Through a structured three-role system, no aspect of care is ever overlooked.

  • Real-time health monitoring & alerts
  • Complete medical history continuity
  • Seamless caretaker-family-doctor coordination
